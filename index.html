Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 and 3/4 cups of flour
  • 3/4 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 and 1/2 tsp baking powder and baking soda
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 cup of boiling water
Time Remaining: 45:00

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F for at least 15mins.
  2. Grease and flour two 9-inch round pans.
  3. place a sieve over a bowl, mix all the dry ingredients.
  4. Mix well after adding eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla to the dry mixture.
  5. Pour in the boiling water and mix well.
  6. Divide the batter between the prepared pans.
  7. Bake for 28-30 minutes.
  8. Then invert to a cooling rack, cool completely before frosting.

Preparation Time: 45 minutes

Servings: 12
